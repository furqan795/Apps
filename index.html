Understanding Your BMI

Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

This may indicate being underweight. Consider consulting a healthcare provider for nutritional advice.

Normal (BMI 18.5-24.9)

Congratulations! This range is associated with the lowest risk of health problems. Maintain your healthy lifestyle.

Overweight (BMI 25-29.9)

This may increase your risk for health problems. Consider healthy eating and increased physical activity.

Obese (BMI 30+)

This increases risk for serious health conditions. Consult a healthcare provider for guidance on weight management.
